Quantum Software Engineering Community

Quantum software will enable complex quantum computing applications. Thus, we need to develop novel quantum software engineering (QSE) methods. To this end, we need to build a multidisciplinary community spanning across software engineering, computer science, quantum computing, quantum physics, and quantum computer systems. This page will be the entry point for such a community.
